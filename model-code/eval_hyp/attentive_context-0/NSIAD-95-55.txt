this report responds to your request that we review the department of the army's disposal program for chemical munitions , materiel , and facilities that are not specifically included in the u.s. stockpile of unitary chemical weapons .
in reviewing the army's nonstockpile chemical disposal program , we interviewed and obtained data from officials of the department of defense ( dod ) , the department of the army , the army chemical demilitarization and remediation activity , the army chemical and biological defense agency , and the u.s. army corps of engineers .
