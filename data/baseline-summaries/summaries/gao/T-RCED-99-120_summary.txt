example , epa estimated that the office of air and radiation's program component for radiation had an enacted fiscal year 1998 budget of $4.6 million . two of these offices — the office of prevention funds would be classified under their various program components . for fiscal year 2000 , epa made several changes to improve the clarity of its budget justification . according to epa officials ,
