( c ) imprisonment of certain violent felons . — ( 1 ) mandatory life imprisonment . — notwithstanding any other provision of law , a person who is convicted in a court of the united states of a serious violent felony shall be sentenced to life imprisonment if — ( a ) the person has been convicted ( and those convictions have become final ) on separate prior occasions in a court of the united states or of a state of — ( i ) 2 or more serious violent felonies ; or ( ii ) one or more serious violent felonies and one or more serious drug offenses ; and ( b ) each serious violent felony or serious drug offense used as a basis for sentencing under this subsection , other than the first , was committed after the defendant's conviction of the preceding serious violent felony or serious drug offense .
