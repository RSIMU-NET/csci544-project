project - based rental assistance ; tenant - based homeownership assistance for first - time homebuyers ; self - sufficiency activities , including escrow savings accounts ; other activities , as specified by the secretary , in support of tenant - based , project - based , or homeownership assistance ; and administrative costs . income eligibility , targeting , subsidy determination , and quality inspection rules would all have been loosened , while portability and enhanced voucher features would have been restricted . the changes in the bill would have been phased in . the secretary was directed to develop temporary implementing regulations within 90 days of passage , and final regulations , not including funding formulas , within 18 months . the secretary was also directed to undertake negotiated rulemaking to develop grant and administrative fee allocation formulas , to be published within 24 months . hearings were held on the slhfa in the house on may 11 , 2005 ;
