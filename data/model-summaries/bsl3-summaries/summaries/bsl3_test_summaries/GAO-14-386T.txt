u.s. customs and border protection ( cbp ) is responsible for enforcing laws prohibiting the illegal importation of goods , including prescription drugs that have not been approved for marketing in the united states by fda .
the department of justice ( doj ) may investigate and prosecute an operator of an internet pharmacy that is suspected of violating federal laws .
u.s. immigration and customs enforcement ( ice ) is responsible for , among other things , investigating violations of customs and trade laws , including those related to trafficking in counterfeit goods .
like brick - and - mortar pharmacies , internet pharmacies are subject to federal and state statutes and regulations that are designed to ensure the safety , efficacy , and proper administration of medications .
